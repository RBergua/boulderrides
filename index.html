Boulder Group Rides

Every group ride you're looking for, all in one place! This site pulls upcoming rides from the most active cycling clubs in Boulder County and displays them on an interactive map.

Why this exists

Boulder's group ride scene is incredible. But finding out what's happening, when, and where usually means checking a dozen club pages. This project puts it all in one place.

A backend process pulls the latest events from the Strava API twice a day (12 PM & 1 AM) and filters them down to rides that begin near Boulder. The data is always fresh and requires zero effort from the clubs themselves.

How to use it

The calendar at the top shows the next 7 days. Days with rides are highlighted in gray. The first day with rides is selected automatically when the page loads. Tap or click any other day to load its routes on the map.

Routes are drawn as solid lines on paved surfaces and dashed lines on unpaved (e.g., gravel) surfaces. A pin marks the start of each ride.

On desktop: hover over a route or its start pin to highlight it, dim all others to gray, and see the ride details. Click to open the Strava event in a new tab.

On mobile: tap a route or its start pin to highlight it, dim all others to gray, and open a panel at the bottom with the ride details and a "View on Strava" button. Swipe the panel down or tap anywhere on the map to dismiss it and restore all routes.

Mobile app

There is no need to download a separate app. This site adapts automatically to phones and tablets. Add it to your home screen for a full-screen, app-like experience.

On Android: open in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right, and select "Add to Home screen".

On iPhone: open in Safari, tap the share button (↑) at the bottom, and select "Add to Home Screen".
